\section{Looking Ahead}

My journey in Japan is still only beginning.

There are many goals I want to achieve, many skills I want to develop, and many experiences that still lie ahead.

This book will continue to document that journey—not only the successes, but also the challenges, mistakes, and lessons learned along the way.

I hope that by sharing my experiences, I can encourage others to believe that meaningful progress is possible through patience, discipline, and continuous learning.
