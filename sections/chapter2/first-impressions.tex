\section{First Impressions of Japan}

Arriving in Japan was both exciting and overwhelming.

Everything felt different. The streets were clean, public transportation was punctual, and people followed rules with remarkable discipline. It was a completely new environment compared to what I had experienced before.

Although I had seen many videos about Japan, experiencing it in person was entirely different. Every train station, every street, and every small detail made me realize that I had truly started a new chapter in my life.

At that moment, I knew there was no turning back. This was the beginning of a new journey.
