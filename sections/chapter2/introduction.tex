\section{The Beginning of My Journey}

Most people begin their journey to Japan because of work, education, or financial opportunities.

Mine began with a Nissan GT-R.

The Nissan GT-R, often called "Godzilla," was the first thing that sparked my curiosity about Japan. At first, I admired it simply as an incredible car. As time passed, my curiosity expanded beyond the car itself and into the country where it was born.

I became fascinated by Japan's discipline, technology, culture, and way of life. Looking back today, I realize that one passion led me toward one of the biggest decisions of my life.
