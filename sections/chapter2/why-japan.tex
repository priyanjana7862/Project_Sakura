\section{Why Japan?}

As I learned more about Japan, I realized that my interest was no longer only about cars.

I admired the country's discipline, cleanliness, safety, and the respect people show toward one another.

Sri Lanka will always be a beautiful country in my heart. However, I felt that the environment around me was not the best place to build the future I wanted. I wanted to live in a society where I could grow through discipline, responsibility, and continuous self-improvement.

Another reason was independence.

I wanted to learn how to stand on my own, make my own decisions, and build a life through my own efforts.

Of course, earning money was also important. However, my goal was never to become rich overnight. I came to Japan to build my future step by step.
