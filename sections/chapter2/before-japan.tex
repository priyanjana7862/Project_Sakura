\section{Life Before Japan}

Before coming to Japan, I was studying to become a software engineer.

My educational background was focused on mathematics and science, including Advanced Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. I later began preparing for a career in software engineering.

Only a few months before completing that path, I made one of the biggest decisions of my life—to leave everything behind and move to Japan.

Some people may think that I abandoned my education, but I never saw it that way.

Knowledge never disappears. I believe I can always continue my software engineering journey in the future. Moving to Japan was not the end of that dream; it was simply a different path toward it.
