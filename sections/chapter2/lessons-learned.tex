\section{Lessons I Learned}

Living in Japan has taught me far more than I expected.

It has shown me how to become independent, manage my daily responsibilities, and build a balanced lifestyle. I have learned the importance of discipline, consistency, and taking responsibility for my own future.

Perhaps the greatest lesson is that personal growth happens when you step outside your comfort zone. Living alone in another country has challenged me in many ways, but it has also helped me become stronger and more confident.

These lessons continue to shape the person I am becoming.
